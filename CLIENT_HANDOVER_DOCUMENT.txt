1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
============================================================

This project is a modern business website for Auxano Solutions Technology Limited. It presents the company as an end-to-end IT solutions provider covering infrastructure, networking, hardware, software, CCTV, access control, managed IT support, and consultancy.

The website is built to do more than show company information. It is designed to generate leads, support sales conversations, educate prospects, and give the client a content system for publishing updates such as blogs, case studies, testimonials, FAQs, and open career roles.

Main business goals:
- Present Auxano Solutions as a professional technology partner.
- Explain services clearly for visitors who may not know the exact solution they need.
- Allow prospects to contact the company, request consultation, and generate estimate ranges.
- Send leads into HubSpot and preserve fallback email notifications.
- Improve search visibility through SEO pages, sitemap, robots file, metadata, Open Graph image, and structured data.
- Allow content updates through the embedded Sanity CMS.

1. Professional responsive website
Usefulness:
- Works across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Gives prospects a polished first impression.
- Helps Auxano look credible before a sales call begins.

2. Services and solutions structure
Usefulness:
- Helps prospects understand Auxano's full offering.
- Allows the sales team to send a direct link to the exact service being discussed.
- Supports SEO for service-related searches.

3. Industry-focused pages
Usefulness:
- Shows that Auxano understands different business environments.
- Makes the website more relevant to prospects in healthcare, education, finance, corporate offices, manufacturing, hospitality, government, retail, real estate, logistics, religious organizations, and multi-site SMEs.

4. Blog system
Usefulness:
- Builds authority and trust.
- Helps the website rank for educational search terms.
- Gives the client a place to publish thought leadership and company updates.

5. Case studies
Usefulness:
- Shows proof of experience and outcomes.
- Helps sales conversations by giving examples of similar work.

6. Lead capture forms
Usefulness:
- Visitors can submit contact and consultation requests.

Usefulness:
- Allows non-technical editors to manage selected content.
- Supports blog posts, case studies, testimonials, FAQs, careers, and other CMS-driven content.

Usefulness:

- Google Analytics 4 can be enabled for deeper traffic analysis.
- Microsoft Clarity can be enabled for heatmaps and visitor behavior recordings.

10. Cloudflare Turnstile
Usefulness:

- Can be enabled by adding the Turnstile site key and secret key.

11. Live chat
Usefulness:
- Tawk.to chat is embedded in the website.
- Visitors can start a live conversation while browsing.

12. WhatsApp contact links
Usefulness:
- Allows prospects to reach sales or support quickly through WhatsApp.
- Helpful for Nigerian business users who prefer WhatsApp communication.

13. SEO setup
Usefulness:
- Metadata, canonical URLs, sitemap, robots file, Open Graph image, and structured data help search engines understand the site.
- Blog, service pages, industry pages, and case studies support long-term organic traffic.

6. HOW TO CHECK WEBSITE LEADS
============================================================

Primary lead destination:
- HubSpot

Lead sources:
- Contact page form
- Book consultation form
- Estimate wizard

Steps to test and check a contact lead:
1. Open https://auxanosolutions.net/contact
2. Fill the form with a test name, company, email, phone, service focus, and message.
3. Submit the form.
4. Log in to HubSpot.
5. Check the configured contact form submissions.
6. Search HubSpot Contacts using the email address submitted.
7. Confirm these fields are captured:
   - Name
   - Company
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Service interest
   - Message
   - Lead source/context

Steps to test and check a consultation lead:
1. Open https://auxanosolutions.net/book-consultation
2. Fill and submit the form.
3. Check HubSpot form submissions or Contacts.
4. Confirm the lead source/context is consultation.

Steps to test and check an estimate lead:
1. Open https://auxanosolutions.net/estimate
2. Complete the estimator steps.
3. Enter lead contact details.
4. Submit and wait for the estimate range.
5. Log in to HubSpot.
6. Check the estimate form submissions or Contacts.
7. Confirm the estimate range and service interest were captured.

Fallback email behavior:
- If HubSpot portal ID or form ID is missing, or if HubSpot submission fails, the website can send an email through Resend.
- The destination inbox is controlled by LEAD_FALLBACK_EMAIL.
- Check the configured fallback inbox and Resend logs if a lead is not visible in HubSpot.

7. HOW TO UPDATE CONTENT
============================================================

Editing content in Sanity:
1. Go to https://auxanosolutions.net/studio
2. Log in with the Sanity account.
3. Select the document type to edit.
4. Make changes.
5. Publish the document.
6. Open the live website page and confirm the update.

Blog posts:
- Create a document under Posts.
- Add title, slug, category, published date, reading time, author, cover image, excerpt, takeaways, and body.
- Publish the post.
- It appears on /blog and /blog/[slug].

Case studies:
- Create or edit a Case Studies document.
- Add title, client, industry, location, summary, challenge, solution, result, metrics, and related services.
- Publish the case study.
- It appears on /case-studies and /case-studies/[slug].

Careers:
- Create a Career Openings document.
- Set Open Position to true to show the job on /careers.
- Set Open Position to false or unpublish to remove it from the live careers page.

Testimonials:
- Create or edit a Testimonials document.
- Add quote, name, role, and company.

FAQs:
- Create or edit an FAQs document.
- Add FAQ ID, order, question, and answer.

Immediate cache refresh:
- The website uses caching for performance.
- If an urgent refresh is needed, call the revalidation API with SANITY_REVALIDATE_SECRET.
- Endpoint: /api/revalidate
